About The Community

DeaverTech is a learning-focused tech community designed to help members grow their technical skills. This includes tutorials, Documentation, hands-on assignments, and community-led dicussions. members are encouraged to practice consistently and share progress.

Onboarding & Certification

New members are onboarded through guided tasks and assignment to help them understand the community workflow. certificates are issued to active participants who consistently submit assignments and demonstrates commitment to learning.

Code of Conduct

All members must maintain respectful communication and uphold ethical behaviour within the community. Harassment, discrimination or misuse of shared resources is not tolerated. Violations may lead to removal

Note: Active members gets certificates.
